# PUBLIC RECORD — TYPOGRAPHY NOTES

## Headline Typeface

BootzyTM

Usage

Section titles  
Editorial headlines  
Pull quotes  
Navigation titles  
Print titles  
Hero statements

Characteristics

Bold  
Graphic  
High personality  
Compressed editorial voice  
Functions as the publication headline system

Design role

Bootzy is the visual voice of the publication.  
It should be used sparingly and with intention.

Large type  
Strong hierarchy  
Generous spacing around headlines

Avoid using Bootzy for long text blocks.

---

## Body Typeface

Newsreader

Usage

Essays  
Captions  
Product descriptions  
Editorial text  
Metadata  
Camera notes  
Artist statements

Characteristics

Highly readable  
Editorial tone  
Newspaper-inspired serif  
Designed for long-form reading  
Quiet supporting voice for the photographs

Design role

Newsreader supports the imagery and editorial layout without competing with the photography or headline typography.

Body text should feel like reading a printed publication.

## Layout Philosophy

Typography should feel like a printed newspaper or magazine layout translated to the web.

Key principles

Large headlines  
Strong negative space  
Clear column rhythm  
Editorial pacing  
Photographs lead the layout

Text should support the images, not compete with them.

---

## Visual References

The typographic atmosphere should feel closer to

Photo essays  
Museum wall text  
Independent newspapers  
Artist publications  
Archival publications  
Editorial magazines

Avoid

Tech startup typography  
Overly minimal SaaS design  
Heavy UI styling  
Trendy web typography

The site should feel like an editorial publication that happens to live on the web.
